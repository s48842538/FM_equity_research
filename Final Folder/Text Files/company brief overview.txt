Centuria Industrial REIT (ASX: CIP) – Overview
Australia’s largest domestic pure-play industrial real estate investment trust (REIT).
managed by Centuria Capital Group and focuses exclusively on high-quality industrial properties across Australia.

Company Details: 
•	Founded: 2002 (originally as 360 Capital Industrial Fund)
•	Headquarters: 2 Chifley Square, Level 41, Chifley Tower, Sydney, NSW, Australia7.
•	IPO: December 13, 20127.
Tenants: CIP’s tenant base includes ASX-listed, national, and multinational companies, with about 93% of income derived from these high-quality tenants.
Occupancy & Lease: The trust maintains high occupancy rates (around 97%) and a long weighted average lease expiry (WALE) of 7.6 years

Key Features:
•	Portfolio: CIP owns 87–89 industrial assets valued at approximately $3.8 billion, primarily located in key urban infill areas with strong access to infrastructure and major population centers
•	90% of its portfolio is concentrated in Australia’s high-performing eastern seaboard industrial markets
•	Sector Diversification: The portfolio includes manufacturing & production (25%), distribution centres (42%), transport logistics (14–15%), data centres (11–12%), and cold storage (7%)
•	Tenant Base: Around 93% of income is derived from ASX-listed, national, and multinational tenants, ensuring stability and diversity
•	Management: The REIT is actively managed, aiming to deliver both income and capital growth to investors
